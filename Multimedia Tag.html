Zara Sa is a Hindi song from the Indian film Jannat: In Search of Heaven....
The song has been sung by Krishnakumar Kunnath (K.K).The music of the song is composed by Pritam Chakraborty and the lyrics are penned by Neelesh Misra and Sayeed Quadri.
The film is directed by Kunal Deshmukh and stars Emraan Hashmi, Sonal Chauhan, Javed Sheikh, Vipin Sharma and Vishal Malhotra.
