Internet of Things(IoT)

The Internet of Things (IoT) is the network of physical devices, vehicles, home appliances, & other embedded with electronics software, sensors, acutators & connectivity.
Which enables these things to connect, collect & exchange data creating oppurtinites for more direct integration of physical world into computer based system this reduces Human Efforts.
